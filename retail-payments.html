What is AppPay?

AppPay is our lastest user-friendly application. With AppPay, you can easily accept payments in real-time using your own smartphone or payment terminal, simply by scanning a QR code or sharing a payment link.

Support Your Customers to pay in 4 Ways

Features

Facilitate Your Operation Flow

All-in-One Account for QR Code Payment Acceptances

Fully integrated with PayDollar payment gateway to accept QR code payments with merchant-presented mode and customer-presented mode;

Support you to accept local and multiple QR payment methods from different countries in ONE SINGLE account.

Payment Link / QR Code Sharing

Allow customization of the invoice no, payment amount, link expiry date, and message when generating a payment link/ QR code to share with your customers through email or instant messenger including LINE, WhatsApp, WeChat, etc. Customers click the payment link or scan qr codes will be redirected to the PayDollar payment page to pay.

Payment Notification and History

Push notifications will be received after successful payments.

A summary of all transactions relating to particular transaction no., payment method and channel, amount, date and time can be viewed from PayDollar AppPay.
