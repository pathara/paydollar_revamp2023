Offer Convenient Payment Options & Enhance Efficiency

Accept over 150 global and local payment methods, with the option to connect to 100+ bank acquirers in Asia, and receive payments from various methods under one single platform. Enjoy the flexibility of choosing local settlement in 11 countries.

Ease Integration & Streamline Payment Journey

Enable seamless integration and simplify the payment process by accepting payments across multiple channels, including online, mobile, retail, chatbot, social commerce platforms, and the metaverse. Utilize AppPay SoftPOS and SmartPOS for face-to-face payment acceptance. Benefit from a comprehensive API and SDK, providing a wide range of payment interface options.

Easy to Scale your Business in APAC

Empower your business to expand into new markets in the APAC region with local acquisition and support. Choose from flexible local settlement options available in 11 countries to help you succeed.

Comprehensive Checkout Platform Plugin Support

We offer comprehensive support for third-party partner booking platform plugins across various industries, including Hotel (Sabre, SiteMinder, D-Edge), eCommerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce), and F&B platforms (OptiTable, Eat 2 Eat).

Gain More Customer Insights & Streamline Account Operation

Access all your sales data from multiple channels in one merchant dashboard. With cross-channel insights and multi-level user administration, you can gain a comprehensive view of your business.

Manage Fraud at Scale
