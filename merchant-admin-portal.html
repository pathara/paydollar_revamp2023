Powerful Merchant Admin Portal

Connect and manage all cross-channel sales and business performance in one portal.

Dashboard

A user-friendly analytics tool that provides real-time analysis of your business, including payment insights from sales summary and transaction volume to consumer buying habits (e.g. preferred payment methods, payment channels, and geographical location).

The portal generates all information and insights you need, so you can evaluate a better marketing plan and offer that works, discover new opportunities, and run your business efficiently.

Sales Summary

  • Provide daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly sales data summaries;
  • Provide a comparison against previous periods. Support a variety of data presentation mode.

Transactions Analysis

  • Provide interactive and historical views of the transaction amount and count for a selected period of time;
  • Data can be filtered by location, payment channel, and status.

Breakdown Analysis

  • Provide insightful summary of the payment details including payer country, payment status, payment method, payment channel and 3-D Secure authentication;
  • Select to display in a bar graph, line graph, or pie chart;
  • Select to compare with previous data over a specified period of time.

Transaction Management

PayDollar captures all the data and information when transactions start.

  • Multi-level user administration control
  • Authorization or sales capture, and post-payment data feed support
  • Real-time void, capture, refund request and sales adjustments
  • Instant payment notification via email

Real-time Report
