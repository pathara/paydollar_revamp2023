About PayDollar

Your Payment Digitalization Partner

PayDollar, a leading payment service and technology provider in Asia since 2000, offers all-in-one award-winning and integrated digital payment services to merchants in Asia and Australia. Covering 150+ payment methods in APAC: Credit/Debit Cards, eBanking/Bank Debit, eWallets/Digital Wallets, Buy Now Pay Later, Over-the-counter and Cryptocurrency; Multi-Channel, Multi-Currency, and Multi-Language payment interface support; Advanced digital payment technologies including Tokenization, Payment Link and QR Code Payment, 3D Secure Authentication, Anti-Fraud Filter and Alerts, Global Partner Platform Plugin, Multi-Level Reporting and Big Data Payment Analytics; Local service and 7x24 technical support across 16 operations in 11 countries in Asia and Australia.

A trusted payment partner to maximize your payment acceptance, sales conversion, revenue and cash flow, and account and IT productivity while minimizing the risk of late or missed payment, financial and fraud loss, and time and manpower overheads.
